Built for a New Era

Purpose-built for 21st-century missions, the S-80 is a state-of-the-art, non-nuclear submarine developed by Navantia for NATO operations.

Equipped with a revolutionary third-generation Air Independent Propulsion (AIP) system, it offers extended underwater endurance, advanced stealth, and formidable firepower—including the unique capability to launch cruise missiles, rare among non-nuclear submarines.
